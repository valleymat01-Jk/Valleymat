Services for farmers

Valley Mat focuses on seasonal needs so you get work done on time without running around to find vehicles.

  • Tractor services for ploughing and field preparation
  • Sprayer vehicles for orchards and crop protection
  • Fruit and vegetable transport during harvest season
  • Load carriers and pickups for construction material and supplies

Each booking is matched with a nearby driver who agrees the rate and timing before confirmation.

  • Local drivers familiar with village roads and fields
  • Flexible hourly, daily, or per-kanal pricing
  • Clear communication on timing and location

How Valley Mat works

1. You contact Valley Mat

Call or WhatsApp with your location, work type and preferred time.

2. We find the right vehicle

Nearby registered owners receive request and confirm availability & price.

3. Work is completed

Driver reaches your field, completes the work, and you pay as agreed.

4. Small commission

A small service commission helps keep Valley Mat running and fair for farmers.
